**Pain:** Users want smarter narratives but resist uploading Omi transcripts or full Apple Health exports to SaaS LLMs.

**Trend (2025–2026):** On-device / local-first narratives in OS health features; EU data-space rhetoric reinforces **minimization**.

**VitaSide fit:** ✅ Local MCP default; Azure contract is **opt-in aggregates only** (see `AZURE-CONTRACT.md`).

**Opportunity:** Audit log line: “accessed N files; nothing left device” as demo trust artifact.

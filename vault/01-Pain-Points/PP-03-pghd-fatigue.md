# PP-03 — PGHD fatigue (clinical side)

**Pain:** Dumping raw journals or 90-day wearable streams into the chart **increases cognitive load** for clinicians (2025 sociotechnical PGHD literature).

**Implication:** Value is in **summarization with boundaries** — weekly trends, top 3 flags, explicit limitations — not full transcripts.

**VitaSide fit:** ✅ Aligns with `clinical_summary` + FHIR bundle philosophy (aggregates, DocumentReference for report).

**Course correction:** Marketing and UI should lead with **“binned for clinic”** not “AI read all your notes.”

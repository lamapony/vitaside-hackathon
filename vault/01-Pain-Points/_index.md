## Opportunities (from pains)

1. **Visit prep as product** — not “another health chat,” but a **3-minute doctor handoff** with questions the patient forgot to ask.
2. **Interpretability moat** — every insight shows dates, excerpts, confidence; beats black-box wellness AI.
3. **Agent-native delivery** — sidecar inside Hermes matches how power users already work (notes + agent), vs standalone app churn.
4. **Interop without raw leak** — FHIR/PGHD export of **aggregates**, not transcripts, aligns with 2025–2026 EHR PGHD guidance.

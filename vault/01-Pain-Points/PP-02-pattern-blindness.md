# PP-02 — Pattern blindness despite tracking

**Pain:** Users collect steps, sleep, voice notes — still cannot answer “does stress lag sleep by 2 days for *me*?”

**Market:** Mood/symptom trackers (Daylio-class) excel at logging, weak at **personal statistics** and **time-lagged** structure.

**VitaSide fit:** ✅ Core value — lag correlations, baselines, what-if on historical series.

**Risk:** Multiple testing / spurious correlations on small N → need FDR/regime messaging (partially in codebase; surface in UI).

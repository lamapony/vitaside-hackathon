# PP-01 — Poor recall at medical visits

**Observed pain:** Patients arrive with feelings (“tired,” “off”) but forget timing, triggers, and what changed since the last visit. Clinicians get minutes to reconstruct months of life context.

**Recent context (2025–2026):**
- PGHD and patient-reported data are encouraged in policy, but **visit time** has not grown; the bottleneck is **structured recall**, not lack of apps.
- Digital therapeutics and symptom checkers help **acute** triage, not **longitudinal** “what happened since March?”

**AI “solutions” that miss:**
- Generic chat: no provenance, no dates tied to *your* vault.
- Portal PDFs: labs only, not lifestyle + subjective signals.

**VitaSide fit:** ✅ Strong — `clinical_summary`, doctor HTML, cited excerpts, suggested questions for the clinician.

**Gap to close:** PDF/print workflow; ritual prompt (“generate handoff 24h before visit”).
